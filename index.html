I am a Researcher at Tencent ARC Lab (2020-now), where I work closely with Dr. Xintao Wang on computer vision research and applications. I also worked with Dr. Yu Li for a period of time at ARC. Prior to joining Tencent, I obtained my master's degree from Fudan University in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Xiangyang Xue.

Research interest

My research interests include computer vision and deep learning, particularly focusing on image/video restoration and enhancement, generation and editing, etc.
